Unix

Architectures

Succeeded on

  • aarch-64le
  • arm-64le
  • chrp-32be
  • mips-64be
  • ppc-64be
  • ppc-64le
  • riscv-64le
  • sun4v-64be
  • x86–32le
  • x86–64le

Did not succeed on

  • arm-64le (cfarm240): configure: machine 'aarch64c-unknown' not recognized

Operating Systems

Succeeded on

  • AIX 7.4
  • AlmaLinux 8.1 (cfarm185 aarch-64le)
    • though build dependencies libid3tag and libmad fail: configure: error: cannot guess build type; you must specify one
  • AlmaLinux 9.4 (cfarm120 ppc-64le)
  • AlpineLinux
  • Archlinux
  • CentOS 7.9 and 8
  • Debian stretch buster bullseye bookworm and trixie
  • FreeBSD 14.1 amd64
  • MacOS X 12.6
  • OpenBSD 7.5
    • though you must configure --without-sunaudio
  • OpenSUSE Leap 15.0 and 15.5
  • Solaris 11.4
  • Ubuntu jammy

Did not succeed on

  • AIX 7.1: Too many compilation problems
