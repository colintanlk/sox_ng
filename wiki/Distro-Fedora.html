Distro Fedora

The source rpm contains a modified version of sox-14.4.2 and some patches.

The modifications are:

  • add a missing copyright statement for libgsm. This has been be put back in sox_ng, thanks.
  • remove lpc10, presumably for its doubtful copyright status. In sox_ng the copyright is fixed thanks to legal advice from the FSF leading to a new upstream version.
